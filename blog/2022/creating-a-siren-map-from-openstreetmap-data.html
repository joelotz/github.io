Creating a Siren Map from OpenStreetMap Data

 January 15, 2022      Stardate: 75506.0     Tagged as: OpenStreetMap(OSM)

I spent a couple hours adding tornado sirens in my town to OpenStreetMap based on open-source city data. Using the OSM query site/tool Overpass-Turbo I queried the data I previously added and formatted it. Here is my query script https://overpass-turbo.eu/s/1f4W.
